My top 3 movies off all time

Children of men (2006)

Tragic story that takes you from "no hope" to "maybe there's still hope for humanity".

It shows all the pain and sacrifice that had to be done in order to reach an objective.

One of the best things of this movie are all the long un-cut shots that had to be very precise.

Dazed and Confused (1993)

Pure Rock and Roll, that's all you need to know.

The Deer Hunter (1978)

The best Robert De Niro performance. It's a very tragic tale about a group of friends

affected by the horrible aftermath of war.
